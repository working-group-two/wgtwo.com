The team and your role

The Infrastructure team at Working Group Two builds, maintains and runs the tools that support the engineering efforts of the rest of the company.  While many take infrastructure for granted, WG2 realizes that the infrastructure work we do is vital for our platform and is a big lever that enables security, speed, agility and scale in the other teams.  


We consist of driven engineers with a range of backgrounds ‚Äì from global giants to small startups.  Our team members work at all levels of our stack and have a large set of skills. Our company tech stack encompasses a wide variety of ‚Äúcloud-native‚Äù tools and frameworks, including: Java, Kotlin, Go, Bazel, Envoy, AWS, and Terraform.  

We may work on our CI/CD system, build dashboards and create alerts out of metrics, develop needed tools or help teams tune and secure their services. Our team also helps ensure services that need more esoteric protocols such as SCTP, RTP and DIAMETER function in a cloud environment.  

Because Working Group Two realizes that good infrastructure is a competitive advantage, we have the freedom to take a proactive approach and can plan longer-term and bigger tasks rather than only reacting to immediate needs.


Working Group Two is growing our security team and currently looking for a Security Engineer to join The Infrastructure Team. Working alongside another security engineer, the role will be tasked with engineering security solutions that are integrated across our cloud infrastructure, product teams, application services, and telco services. This includes leveraging the kill chain to build solutions, implement countermeasures, and create alerts to identify, prevent, and contain security events. We aim at leveraging open source solutions and building our own tools for ensuring security. This role is exciting and we hope that you are eager and passionate about creating security solutions in this space.



In this role you will: 


  • Adopt and build security solutions for identifying and preventing misconfigurations across AWS and Kubernetes.
  • Implement a SAST, DAST, Fuzzing across application and infrastructure services.
  • Identify attack vectors across our APIs, Applications, and Telco Services through Penetration Testing and automation.
  • Make security an integral part of our CI/CD pipeline.
  • Provide security guidance to Engineering and Product teams through threat modeling.
  • Manage vulnerabilities across our infrastructure and applications.
  • Build security metrics and alerts.
  • Build telco-facing security solutions using WG2 APIs.


We believe you have: 


  • Security experience.
  • Software development experience in Go, Python or Kotlin.
  • Have a strong understanding of web application security, including hands-on penetration.
  • Have familiarity with secure development practices and security testing techniques (SAST, DAST, fuzzing, etc.).
  • Experience with cloud and container security.



It‚Äôs beneficial if you have: 


  • Kubernetes experience.
  • Telecommunications experience.
  • Interest in machine learning and data science.


This role is based in either of our locations in Oslo, Trondheim, Stockholm, remotely in Germany, but we are open to alternatives.
